Causes

The causes of not protecting our environment in future may lead to :

  • Less trees will be present therefore less oxygen to all people
  • If much greenhouse gases are used e.g. CO2, CH4, etc. they are poisonus to us.
  • If we wont conserve water for our future use then we might run into water problems.
  • Burning factory produced things releases harmful chemicals into the air
